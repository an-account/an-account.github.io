FAQs

Got some questions?

How do I get started?

Super simple, you need to be an admin in your chosen CRM or service desk. Click here to get started.

What if I have users that send few messages?

Good news, we credit you for users who send less than 100 messages in a billing month - even if you have an annual subscription.

Can I invite my agents to see their performance?

Sure, after you've integrated with your service desk - just click 'Invite Agents' in the navigation. They'll receive an invitation to choose a password and login.

What if one of my agents leaves my company?

We'll issue you a credit for any unused time.

What if I recruit a new agent?

We'll charge you pro-rata for the remainder of that billing cycle.
