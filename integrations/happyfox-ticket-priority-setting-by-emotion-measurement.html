What is Happyfox priority setting?

In Happyfox, you can set the priority of tickets. Such priority setting enables you to group tickets, and other actions based on it.

How does GreenflyAI automate ticket priority setting in Happyfox?

We use emotion measurement powered by AI. This enables GreenflyAI to automatically set the priority of tickets in Happyfox.
